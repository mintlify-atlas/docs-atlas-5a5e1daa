## What makes it special

### Hand-picked color palette

Every color in Shades of Purple has been carefully selected to work together harmoniously. The purple tones provide a unique, eye-catching aesthetic while maintaining excellent readability for all syntax elements.

### Trusted by millions

With over 5 million users worldwide, Shades of Purple is one of the most downloaded themes on the VS Code marketplace. The theme has earned 175+ five-star ratings from developers who love it.

### Built for productivity

The theme's color choices help you focus on what matters. Important syntax elements stand out clearly, while comments and secondary elements fade into the background just enough to reduce visual noise.

### Free and open source

Shades of Purple is completely free to use and open source under the MIT license. You can use it in personal projects, commercial work, and even fork it to create your own variant.
